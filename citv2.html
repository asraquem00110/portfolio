× CASH IN TRANSIT SYSTEM v2 using MEVN stack

DESCRIPTION:

  • Cash-in-transit (CIT) is the physical transfer of banknotes or coins from one location to another.
  • A system that helps cit department to monitor the status of acu trip.
  • Prepare Cash Request.
  • Clustering of sites for the preparation of Dispatch Order.
  • Assigning Dispatch Order to specific Personnel.
  • Generate reports and dashboard monitoring.

TECHNOLOGY USED:

  • NODEJS || EXPRESS || MONGODB || NOSQL || HTML || CSS || BOOTSTRAP || JAVASCRIPT|| VUEJS
  • IONIC || CAPACITOR
  • REST PROTOCOL || LEAFLETJS
